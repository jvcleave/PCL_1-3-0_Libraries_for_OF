\section{Edge Ratio}

The edge ratio of a tetrahedron is: 
\[
\frac{L_{\max}}{L_{\min}}.
\]

\tetmetrictable{edge ratio}%
{$1$}%                  Dimension
{$[1,3]$}%              Acceptable range
{$[1,DBL\_MAX]$}%       Normal range
{$[1,DBL\_MAX]$}%       Full range
{$1$}%                  Equilateral tet
